ABOUT US



The estimated results show that about 18 million senior citizens are below the poverty line. And most of them are facing humiliation and abuse due to being dependent on their young ones. Apart from these issues, they further face loneliness, as every member of the family is busy with their own works. So to make them too indulge with their works our organization allows elders for the day-stay, which may cheer them. The homeless seniors will get home and the lonely ones will get some friends. And if you donate for them, you would be blessed. Be a part of the grooming steps for the senior citizens with several upcoming fortunate by their blessings.
